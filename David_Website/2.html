ABOUT ME

I'm a student in SIVA

I am now a sophomore in SIVA, majoring in interactive design in art and technology. Taking pictures and cycling are part of my life. Going out and playing, no matter where I go, I take a camera and shoot everything about life. As an art student, I hope that my design can be derived from life and use in life. I feel that the design that is out of life is a failure and useless. I am eager to explore my unknown field and love those tech gadgets. This is me.
